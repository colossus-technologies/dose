  1  ''' 
  2  Loose Circular Brainfuck (LCBF) Interpreter 
  3  Date created: 15th August 2012 
  4   
  5  LCBF uses all 8 operations in standard Brainfuck with the  
  6  following differences: 
  7      1. The tape or array is circular (a ring list) instead of  
  8      linear. When the pointer is at the "end" of the tape, an  
  9      increment (">") will move the tape to the start. Similarly,  
 10      when the pointer is decremented at the "beginning" of the  
 11      tape, the pointer goes to the end. 
 12      2. Operations after a start loop operator ("[") will only be  
 13      executed provided the loop(s) are properly closed. If the  
 14      loops are open, the program will terminate. 
 15      3. However, it is possible to have an end loop operator ("]")  
 16      without a preceding start loop operator ("["). In this case,  
 17      the end loop operator ("]") will be ignored and execution continues. 
 18      4. Unclosed or unopened loops may result in non-deterministic  
 19      behaviour. 
 20      5. All inputs are pre-defined at the start of the program. 
 21       
 22  @see: http://esolangs.org/wiki/Loose_Circular_Brainfuck_(LCBF) 
 23  '''
